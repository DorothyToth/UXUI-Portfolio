about me

As a dynamic, independent creative director, I pride myself in providing smart, meaningful, and unique design that solves problems and adds value. With over twenty years of experience in both the advertising and educational publishing industries, I am grateful to share my passion and expertise as a user champion, creative partner, and brand expert.
